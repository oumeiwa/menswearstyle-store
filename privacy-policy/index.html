Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how MenswearStyle.store and the internal Google Ads campaign workflow tool handle Google OAuth authorization and Google Ads API data.

Developer

This website and developer project are maintained by Lian Shuai, an independent developer.

Google OAuth Usage

The internal tool uses Google OAuth 2.0 so authorized users can grant access to approved Google Ads accounts. The tool does not request or store Google passwords.

OAuth access is requested only for the Google Ads API functionality needed to support internal campaign workflow, validation, management and reporting.

Google Ads API Data Usage

Google Ads API access is used only for internal campaign workflow activities for authorized Google Ads accounts. Supported activities include Search Campaign creation, campaign management and reporting.

Campaign Data Handling

Campaign data may be used to prepare campaign structures, validate campaign settings, deploy approved Search Campaign changes and generate performance reporting for authorized accounts.

Account Authorization

The tool is intended for internal users only. Access is limited to Google Ads accounts that have been explicitly authorized through OAuth 2.0.

Data Retention

Campaign workflow data is retained only as needed for internal validation, management and reporting purposes. Authorization can be revoked through the user's Google account settings.

Data Sharing

Google Ads account data and campaign data are not sold, rented, or shared for unrelated advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing. Data is used only for the internal workflow described on this website.

Limited Use

Use of Google user data is limited to the practices disclosed in this policy and to the internal Google Ads workflow functionality requested by authorized users.

Access Revocation And Deletion

Users can revoke OAuth access from their Google account settings. To request deletion of stored workflow records associated with an authorized account, contact chesticksantbab@gmail.com.

Security Limits

  • No Google password storage.
  • No scraping.
  • No unauthorized account access.
  • No sale of Google Ads account or campaign data.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact chesticksantbab@gmail.com.
