The Most Useful Chino Colors

Which chino colors build the most outfits and how to choose a cut that works with sneakers and leather shoes.

In This Guide

  1. Overview
  2. What To Check
  3. Common Mistakes
  4. Buying Checklist

Overview

Stone, olive, navy and khaki are the most useful chino colors because they work with both light and dark tops.

Fabric should have enough structure to avoid looking like lounge pants. A little weight helps chinos hold a cleaner line.

What To Check

Fit should be tapered but not tight. Chinos need to move between sneakers, loafers and casual boots.

Avoid buying too many near-identical beige pairs. Different color families give you more outfit range.

Common Mistakes

The easiest mistake is buying the idea of an item instead of judging how it works on your body, in your climate and with your existing wardrobe.

Another mistake is ignoring maintenance. Even strong menswear looks weak when it is wrinkled, dry, misshapen or paired with neglected shoes.

Buying Checklist

  • Useful neutral color
  • Structured cotton twill
  • Clean taper
  • Hem suits shoes
  • Works with jackets and knits

Bottom Line

The Most Useful Chino Colors should help you make a more confident purchase or styling decision. Choose the version that fits your real routine, works with pieces you already own and can be maintained properly.
