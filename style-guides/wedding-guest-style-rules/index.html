Wedding Guest Style Rules For Men

How to respect the dress code, venue and season while keeping the outfit tasteful.

In This Guide

  1. Overview
  2. What To Check
  3. Common Mistakes
  4. Buying Checklist

Overview

Start with the invitation. Dress codes, venue and time of day tell you more than any generic rule.

Navy and mid-gray suits cover most weddings. Lighter colors work for daytime or warm-weather events, while darker suits suit evening settings.

What To Check

Avoid outshining the wedding party. Fit, clean shoes and tasteful accessories matter more than loud patterns.

If the dress code is unclear, ask rather than guess. Being slightly overdressed is usually safer than being underdressed.

Common Mistakes

The easiest mistake is buying the idea of an item instead of judging how it works on your body, in your climate and with your existing wardrobe.

Another mistake is ignoring maintenance. Even strong menswear looks weak when it is wrinkled, dry, misshapen or paired with neglected shoes.

Buying Checklist

  • Dress code respected
  • Seasonal fabric
  • Shoes are clean
  • Accessories are restrained
  • Outfit does not upstage couple

Bottom Line

Wedding Guest Style Rules For Men should help you make a more confident purchase or styling decision. Choose the version that fits your real routine, works with pieces you already own and can be maintained properly.
