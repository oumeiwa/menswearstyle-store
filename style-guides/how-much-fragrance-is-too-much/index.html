How Much Fragrance Is Too Much?

How to wear fragrance in a way that feels considered instead of overwhelming.

In This Guide

  1. Overview
  2. What To Check
  3. Common Mistakes
  4. Buying Checklist

Overview

Fragrance should be discovered at conversational distance. If people notice it before they are near you, it is probably too much.

Two sprays are enough for most offices and dinners. Strong scents, warm weather and small rooms all require restraint.

What To Check

Apply to clean skin and avoid spraying clothing heavily. Some fragrances stain fabric or linger longer than intended.

Choose lighter scents for daytime and heavier scents only when the setting supports them.

Common Mistakes

The easiest mistake is buying the idea of an item instead of judging how it works on your body, in your climate and with your existing wardrobe.

Another mistake is ignoring maintenance. Even strong menswear looks weak when it is wrinkled, dry, misshapen or paired with neglected shoes.

Buying Checklist

  • Two to three sprays maximum
  • Setting considered
  • Applied to skin
  • Not sprayed heavily on clothes
  • Season suits scent

Bottom Line

How Much Fragrance Is Too Much? should help you make a more confident purchase or styling decision. Choose the version that fits your real routine, works with pieces you already own and can be maintained properly.
